About Me

My focus over the last 5 years has been primarily front end development. Specifically, architecting single page applications with frameworks that leverage build tools to concatenate and minify sources in addition to running unit tests and other build processes. I'm also relied upon to lead and mentor a team of developers and student developers at Boise State University. I've become practiced at communicating complicated ideas and helping other learn.

While I have contributed to a number of projects with varying backend technologies (.NET, Node.JS, PHP, Python), my passion lies with the browser and emerging front-end technologies. I thrive in environments where I'm able to architect front-end solutions and create testable design and UX.

I am truly excited to work everyday continuing to grow in the craft. I would like to create products I care about across all devices and screen sizes. I'm excited about the reach frameworks like Angular and React give developers. I'm also looking forward to emerging web features in Progressive Web Applications. I look forward to evolving with the growing complexity of front-end development.
